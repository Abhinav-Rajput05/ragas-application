NovaTech Solutions - Company Overview

NovaTech Solutions is a cloud software company founded in 2018, headquartered in Austin, Texas.
The company specializes in AI-powered analytics tools for small and medium businesses.

Products:
- NovaDash: A real-time business intelligence dashboard that connects to over 50 data sources.
- NovaBot: An AI chatbot for customer support that reduces ticket resolution time by 40%.
- NovaShield: A cybersecurity monitoring tool that detects threats using machine learning.

Pricing:
- NovaDash starts at $99/month for up to 5 users.
- NovaBot is priced at $199/month with unlimited conversations.
- NovaShield costs $149/month per 100 endpoints monitored.

Team:
- CEO: Sarah Chen, former VP of Engineering at Salesforce.
- CTO: Marcus Rivera, PhD in Machine Learning from MIT.
- The company has 120 full-time employees across engineering, sales, and support.

Financials:
- NovaTech raised $25 million in Series B funding in 2023.
- Annual recurring revenue reached $8 million in 2024.
- The company serves over 1,200 customers across 30 countries.

Customer Support:
- Support is available 24/7 via live chat and email.
- Average response time is under 2 hours for all paid plans.
- Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager.

NovaTech's mission is to make enterprise-grade analytics accessible to every business,
regardless of size or technical expertise.
